Welcome! If I had to guess, I'd say you're here because you're interested in buying, selling, or trading albums from the venerable Blue Note jazz record label.

A long-time jazz lover, I purchased my first Blue Note album from a used record store in Philadelphia over twenty-five years ago, and I've been collecting them ever since.

Take a look around the site and feel free to contact me if you have any Blue Note records you're interested in selling or trading. Thanks for stopping by!

Blue Note Records was founded on January 6, 1939, when a German immigrant and passionate Jazz fan named Alfred Lion produced his first recording session in New York City.

Blue Note has gone on to represent The Finest In Jazz, tracing the entire history of the music from Hot Jazz, Boogie Woogie, and Swing, through Bebop, Hard Bop, Post Bop, Soul Jazz, Avant-Garde, and Fusion, and into Jazz’s numerous modern day incarnations under the leadership of Bruce Lundvall, who revived Blue Note in 1984, and the label’s current President, Don Was, who took the helm in 2012.
